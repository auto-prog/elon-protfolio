ABOUT ME

Elon Musk is a visionary entrepreneur, engineer, and industrial designer known for founding and leading multiple groundbreaking companies. He is the CEO and lead designer of SpaceX, CEO and product architect of Tesla, Inc., founder of Neuralink and The Boring Company, and owner of X (formerly Twitter). Musk’s work spans space exploration, electric vehicles, artificial intelligence, brain–computer interfaces, and infrastructure innovation. Driven by an ambitious mission to "make life multiplanetary" and accelerate humanity’s transition to sustainable energy, Musk is widely recognized as one of the most influential figures in tech and innovation.
